Forest Ecological Conservation


Nowadays, The commercial development of human beings has threatened the existence of forests.
If this continues, it will not only cause disasters to the earth's environment, but also affect the
survival of human beings.

There are some ways you can protect forests:
  1. Plant New Trees
    2. Planting trees is the easiest way to conserve forests. By planting trees, the area of the forest can be expanded and the forest can return to its original appearance.
  2. Buy Rain Forest Alliance Certified Products
    3. Buying Rain Forest Alliance Certified Products is also one of the useful method to protect forests, there are some organizations that can help to support the world forests.
    1. Plant a Tree Today
    2. World Wide Fund for Nature
    3. One Tree Planted
  3. Use Tree-Free Products
    4. Use Tree-Free Products can can reduce the number of felled trees, thus protecting the ecology of the forest.
