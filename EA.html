Why need to promote environmental protection?

Have you noticed that the earth is become more and more hot or there is more extreme weather and disasters?
That is because the pollution of our human development has affected the ecology of the earth and made the beautiful earth sick!
If we don't do anythings to protect and repair the environment of our earth, we humans will have a hard time suriving.

How to promote environmental protection?

We can start with ourself, there are few method:
  1. Do not use plastic cutlery as less as possible
  2. Take public transportation to word or to school as much as possible
  3. Conserve energy in your daily life
  4. Buying reusable items
  5. Reduce br reusing
